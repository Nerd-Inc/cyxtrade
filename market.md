# CyxTrade Market & Monetization

## Revenue Model

### 1. Core Transaction Fee
- Charge fee on completed trades.
- Recommended launch pricing: `0.3% - 0.8%` on advertiser/trader side.
- Keep buyer side at `0%` initially to improve conversion.

### 2. Trader Subscription (Upsell)
- Offer paid tiers for active traders.
- Suggested range: `$29 - $99/month`.
- Include benefits such as:
  - Better listing/ranking visibility
  - Higher limits
  - Faster support
  - Advanced analytics/API access

### 3. Add-On Fees
- Dispute/arbitration fee (preferably loser pays).
- Priority settlement fee.
- Instant payout fee.
- Fiat on-ramp/off-ramp markup (when rails are integrated).

### 4. B2B & Platform Services
- White-label offering for remittance operators/partners.
- API access pricing:
  - Monthly platform fee
  - Usage-based fee per transaction

## Example Revenue Projection
- Monthly GMV: `$2,000,000`
- Average take rate: `0.6%`
- Gross platform revenue: `$12,000/month` (before operating costs)

## Pricing Principles
- Start with low-friction pricing to grow liquidity.
- Monetize power users (traders) more than retail buyers.
- Keep pricing corridor-aware (different rates by pair/market depth).
- Review fee bands monthly based on volume and churn.
